Welcome

what's all this about anyway?

This is for the list-makers, the note-takers, the post-it pilots, the track-keepers, and the dabbling doodlers. It?s for those who feel that there are few platforms as powerful as a blank page, and for those who love that simple act of putting pen to paper. But mostly, Bullet Journal is for the multi-taskers who need to keep track of it all, at the speed of life. So what is Bullet Journal? At it?s core, it?s a powerful life tracking system. Bullet Journal provides a simple and flexible framework that will help you record the past, manage the present, and plan for the future. As every person?s life is different, the Bullet Journal is also design to be modular, allowing you pick and choose the techniques that will be most helpful for you. To get started, all you need is a dedicated notebook and something to write with.
